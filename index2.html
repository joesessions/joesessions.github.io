When I worked with Health Risk Screening, Inc., the company consisted of two founders: Karen McGowan and her son, Eric Green. Karen, in her long career of healthcare, had been developing a tool which caregivers used to give scores to their customers. The tool was originally a paper form which caregivers filled in to produce a score. If properly used, the tool avoids preventable deaths.

I then took Karen through every path on each of the twenty-two decision trees to ensure that I had not altered the intent on each area. Currently the HRST is utilized by Intellectual and Developmental Disabilities and Behavioral Health Divisions in nine states and over 2,000 private provider agencies in the United States.
